Frequently Asked Questions
Where do I get my section code?
Your instructor will provide the section code on your syllabus or verbally during the first class. It typically looks like CS101, MATH201, etc.
Can I enroll in multiple sections?
Yes. Send a separate JOIN message for each section code. Example: JOIN CS101 then JOIN MATH201.
Will I be charged for these messages?
PAWS Alert is free from Pellissippi State. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.
How do I unsubscribe?
Reply STOP to any message. You will immediately stop receiving alerts and receive one final confirmation. Reply JOIN [CODE] to re-enroll at any time.
Who do I contact for help?
Reply HELP to any message, or email pawsalert@pstcc.edu.
